# Policies

Church policies provide practical guidance for ministry operations, safety, conduct, and administration.

These policies exist to promote consistency, accountability, and care in the life of the church while supporting its mission and values.

---

## Purpose of Policies

Policies are adopted to:

- Provide clear expectations and procedures  
- Protect individuals and the church body  
- Support safe and effective ministry  
- Ensure responsible stewardship of resources

---

## Policy Authority

Policies are approved by the appropriate governing body of the church and are subject to review and revision as needed.

All policies must be consistent with the Constitution and Bylaws.
