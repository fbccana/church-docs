## Position Summary

Responsible for the general maintenance and cleanliness of the church property, serving as the primary custodial staff member with specialized responsibilities for baptistery preparation and building security operations.

## Essential Functions and Responsibilities

### Primary Responsibilities (70-80% of time)

- Maintain floors, dust furniture and equipment, wash walls and windows, vacuum and shampoo carpets throughout assigned areas
- Clean restrooms and replenish supplies on daily basis
- Clean assigned areas according to established schedule
- Open and close building daily as scheduled, ensuring proper security protocols
- Prepare baptistery for use as directed and clean following use

### Secondary Responsibilities (15-20% of time)

- Move furniture, set up tables and chairs for suppers, banquets, and other occasions
- Set up assembly and classroom areas for regular activities as assigned
- Assist in kitchen operations as scheduled
- Clean church entrance, sidewalk and parking areas

### Additional Responsibilities (5-10% of time)

- Assist with wedding facility arrangements when requested (extra compensation per church policy)
- Coordinate with Facilities Manager for cleaning and maintenance supply needs
- Other duties as assigned by Minister of Administration

## Qualifications

### Required

- High school diploma or equivalent
- 1-2 years of custodial or maintenance experience preferred
- Ability to operate basic cleaning equipment and machinery
- Reliable and punctual with strong attention to detail
- Physical ability to lift up to 50 pounds and perform manual labor

### Preferred

- Experience in church or institutional facility maintenance
- Basic knowledge of HVAC and building systems
- Customer service skills for interaction with church members

## Physical Requirements and Working Conditions

- Extended periods of standing, walking, bending, and lifting
- Exposure to cleaning chemicals and equipment
- Indoor and outdoor work environments
- Flexible schedule including evenings and weekends as needed
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision

## Performance Expectations

- Maintain high standards of cleanliness and facility appearance
- Complete assigned tasks efficiently within established timeframes
- Respond promptly to special event setup and cleaning requests
- Demonstrate reliability in building security responsibilities

## Reporting Relationships

**Direct Reports:** None  
**Key Internal Relationships:** Facilities Manager, Church Secretary, Ministry Staff  
**External Relationships:** Vendors, Service Contractors
