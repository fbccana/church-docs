## Position Summary

Responsible for the specialized cleaning and maintenance of children's areas and nursery facilities, ensuring a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for families and young children.

## Essential Functions and Responsibilities

### Primary Responsibilities (70-80% of time)
- Maintain children's areas and nursery rooms including thorough cleaning and sanitization
- Clean and disinfect toys, furniture, and child-specific equipment according to health standards
- Vacuum, mop, and maintain floors in children's areas with special attention to safety
- Clean restrooms and replenish supplies in children's areas

### Secondary Responsibilities (15-20% of time)
- Dust furniture and equipment, wash walls and windows in assigned areas
- Set up and arrange children's areas for special programs and events
- Maintain supply inventory for children's area cleaning materials
- Support overall church cleanliness standards in common areas as needed

### Additional Responsibilities (5-10% of time)
- Assist with wedding facility arrangements when requested (extra compensation per church policy)
- Coordinate with Facilities Manager for specialized cleaning and maintenance supplies
- Other duties as assigned by Minister of Administration

## Qualifications

### Required
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Experience working with or around children preferred
- Knowledge of proper sanitization and safety procedures for children's environments
- Attention to detail and understanding of cleanliness standards for child care facilities
- Flexible schedule accommodating second job responsibilities

### Preferred
- Experience in child care facility maintenance or school custodial work
- Basic understanding of child safety requirements
- Customer service skills for interaction with families

## Physical Requirements and Working Conditions
- Extended periods of standing, walking, bending, and detailed cleaning work
- Exposure to child-safe cleaning chemicals and sanitizing products
- Indoor work environment focused on children's areas
- Preferred schedule: Monday-Friday, 4 hours per day (flexible based on availability)
- Ability to maintain child-focused safety and cleanliness standards

## Performance Expectations
- Maintain children's areas to exceptional cleanliness and safety standards
- Demonstrate reliability and consistency in part-time schedule
- Ensure proper sanitization protocols for child safety
- Support children's ministry programs with efficient facility preparation

## Reporting Relationships
**Direct Reports:** None  
**Key Internal Relationships:** Facilities Manager, Lead Custodian, Children's Ministry Staff  
**External Relationships:** Child-Safe Cleaning Supply Vendors

## Compensation and Benefits
Hourly wage as determined by Personnel Committee and approved in annual church budget. Additional compensation available for wedding assistance per church policy.

## Employment Conditions
- Position continues until terminated by mutual agreement or by request of Minister of Administration
- Two weeks' notice required for resignation unless otherwise mutually agreed
- Subject to successful completion of background check and child protection screening
- Compliance with church policies and child protection procedures required
- At-will employment relationship
