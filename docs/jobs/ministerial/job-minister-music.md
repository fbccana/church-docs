## Position Summary

To coordinate and plan the church music program and worship for FBC Corsicana. The Minister of Music/Worship will lead a ministry that includes praise teams, choirs, and various other music programs. The purpose of the Music/Worship Ministry is to lead the entire congregation to glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus through musical worship.

## Essential Functions and Responsibilities

### Music & Worship Leadership (60% of time)

-   Lead a comprehensive Music/Worship Ministry for all ages.
-   Coordinate and recruit volunteers for the church music program.
-   Plan and prepare weekly Sunday worship services in conjunction with the Senior Pastor.
-   Engage in personal visitation and evangelism as assigned by the Senior Pastor.

### Administration & Planning (40% of time)

-   Prepare and implement an annual calendar of activities, to further the mission of the Music Ministry.
-   Facilitate in advertising music programs to the church and to the community.
-   Attend weekly staff meetings.
-   Prepare and implement an annual budget for the Music/Worship ministry.
-   Serve alongside the other staff members harmoniously in leading FBC Corsicana to discern and do the will of God.
-   Work with any assigned church ministry teams and committees to aid their ministry effectiveness.

## Qualifications

### Required

-   Has a clear call from God.
-   Has demonstrated a solid testimony of personal salvation through Jesus Christ and promotes a positive, Christian attitude in all work activities.
-   Maintains a growing spiritual life with Christ through ongoing prayer, study of God's Word and service to others.
-   Has a minimum of 3 years experience as a Music/Worship Minister.
-   Has the ability to listen and communicate clearly, both orally and in written form.
-   Has excellent interpersonal skills, effective speaking skills and the ability to speak confidently before groups.
-   Is competent using or willing to learn computer software.
-   Is in sufficient good health to discharge duties properly with or without reasonable accommodations.
-   Has a record of effective service in Music/Worship Ministry.
-   Has a bachelor's degree or higher in music from an accredited school.
-   Is a seminary graduate.
-   Demonstrates a high level of administrative, organizational and leadership skills.
-   Will support the traditional Baptist interpretation of the Bible as found in the 1963 Baptist Faith and Message.
-   Passes a criminal, financial, and driving background check as well as a drug screening.
-   Complies with all applicable federal and state laws.
-   Be responsible for obtaining the correct driver's license for driving the church bus.
