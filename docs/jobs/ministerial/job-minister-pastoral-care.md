# Job Description for the Position of Minister of Pastoral Care

**First Baptist Church, Corsicana**  
**June 8, 2011**

## General Objective

To develop and implement a pastoral care ministry that will serve the entire FBC Corsicana family.

## Purpose

Our Pastoral Care Ministry will oversee the loving care of all members of FBC Corsicana. It will especially focus on our people who are home-bound, elderly, hospitalized, widowed, or residing in a nursing home.

## Responsible to

Senior Pastor

## We desire a candidate who...

- Has a clear call from God.
- Has demonstrated a solid testimony of personal salvation through faith in Jesus Christ and moral uprightness.
- Maintains a growing spiritual life with Christ through ongoing prayer, study of God's Word, and service to others.
- Has a minimum of 3 years experience/ministry in Pastoral Care.
- Has quality leadership skills.
- Has the ability to listen and to communicate clearly, both orally and in written form.
- Has effective speaking skills and the ability to speak confidently before groups.
- Is in sufficient good health to discharge duties properly with or without reasonable accommodations.
- Has a bachelor's degree and seminary degree (helpful but not required) and is licensed and ordained by a Baptist church.
- Passes a criminal, financial, and driving background check as well as a drug screening.
- Complies with all applicable federal and state laws.

## Responsibilities

- Communicate with the Senior Pastor the needs of the church members who need Pastoral Care.
- Exemplify the love of Christ to people who suffer from illness, disease, or accidents.
- Equip and lead FBC Corsicana to develop and implement in-reach ministry to current church members.
- Reach out to FBC Corsicana through personal visits or by telephone.
- Visit and minister to the needs of church members who are hospitalized.
- Equip and lead FBC Corsicana to minster to the needs of "shut-ins".
- Help the church remain informed of all members who are in need of pastoral care.
- Work hand in hand with the Deacon body to accomplish a full ministry to widows.
- Serve alongside the other staff members harmoniously in leading FBC Corsicana to discern and do the will of God.
- Attend weekly staff meetings.
- Work with any assigned church ministry teams and committees to aid their ministry effectiveness.
- Comply with all established church policies and procedures.
- Be prepared to perform outside of this job description in any way the Senior Pastor deems necessary.

---

*This is a full-time exempt position.*
