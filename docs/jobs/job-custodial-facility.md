## Position Summary

Responsible for the comprehensive management of church facilities, grounds, and equipment, overseeing maintenance operations and coordinating with custodial staff to ensure optimal facility conditions for all church activities and programs.

## Essential Functions and Responsibilities

### Primary Responsibilities (60-70% of time)

- Oversee maintenance and care of all church buildings, physical structures, parking lots, sidewalks, and contents
- Manage church vehicle maintenance including cleaning, fueling, repairs, and scheduling
- Coordinate and perform painting and care of interior and exterior surfaces
- Operate and maintain heating, cooling, and other building systems according to schedule
- Supervise grounds maintenance including landscaping, mowing, and exterior upkeep

### Secondary Responsibilities (20-30% of time)

- Coordinate custodial supply inventory and procurement for all facility needs
- Arrange facility setups for regular and special church activities
- Manage custodial services coordination for weddings, funerals, and special events
- Prepare annual budget recommendations for Building, Grounds, & Transportation section
- Ensure security protocols including building access and locking procedures

### Additional Responsibilities (10% of time)

- Train and mentor maintenance assistant (Gym/Family Life Center Custodian)
- Coordinate with Minister of Administration and Church Secretary for special assignments
- Oversee contractor relationships and service agreements
- Other duties as assigned by Minister of Administration

## Qualifications

### Required

- High school diploma or equivalent; technical training preferred
- 3-5 years of facilities management or building maintenance experience
- Knowledge of HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and general building systems
- Supervisory or leadership experience
- Valid driver's license and ability to operate church vehicles

### Preferred

- Associate degree in facilities management, engineering, or related field
- Experience in church or institutional facility management
- HVAC certification or related technical certifications
- Budget development and management experience

## Physical Requirements and Working Conditions

- Ability to perform manual labor including lifting up to 75 pounds
- Indoor and outdoor work in various weather conditions
- Use of power tools, maintenance equipment, and machinery
- Flexible schedule including evenings and weekends as needed
- Ability to respond to emergency facility situations

## Performance Expectations

- Maintain all church facilities in excellent condition and appearance
- Manage facility budgets effectively and recommend cost-saving measures
- Operate within approved budget and follow established purchasing procedures
- Coordinate seamlessly with custodial staff and church leadership
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and building codes
- Respond promptly to maintenance emergencies and special requests

## Reporting Relationships

**Direct Reports:** None (coordinates with all custodial staff)  
**Key Internal Relationships:** Minister of Administration, Church Secretary, All Ministry Staff, Custodial Team  
**External Relationships:** Contractors, Vendors, Service Providers, Equipment Suppliers

## Compensation and Benefits

Hourly wage and benefits package as determined by Personnel Committee and approved in annual church budget.

## Employment Conditions

- Position continues until terminated by mutual agreement or by request of Minister of Administration
- Two weeks' notice required for resignation unless otherwise mutually agreed
- Subject to successful completion of background check
- Compliance with church policies and procedures required
- At-will employment relationship
