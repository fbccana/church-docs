# Job Descriptions

This section outlines the roles, responsibilities, and expectations for staff members and key volunteer positions within the church.

Clear job descriptions support effective ministry by defining authority, accountability, and scope of service.

---

## Purpose

Job descriptions are provided to:

- Clarify responsibilities and reporting relationships  
- Establish expectations for service and conduct  
- Support evaluation and accountability  
- Promote unity and effectiveness in ministry

---

## Applicability

These descriptions apply to paid staff and designated volunteer roles as approved by church leadership.

Job descriptions may be reviewed and updated as ministry needs change.
