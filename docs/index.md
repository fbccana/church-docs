# Church Governance Documents

Welcome to the official repository for the governance documents of the church.

This site serves as a centralized reference for the church’s **constitution**, **bylaws**, **policies**, and **job descriptions**. These documents exist to support faithful ministry, ensure good order, and provide clarity for leadership, staff, and members.

---

## Purpose of This Site

The documents published here are intended to:

- Promote transparency and accountability in church governance  
- Provide clear guidance for leadership roles and responsibilities  
- Preserve governing documents in a stable, accessible format  
- Serve as a living reference that can be updated as the church grows

Unless otherwise noted, these documents represent the current, adopted policies and governing standards of the church.

---

Use the navigation menu to access the following sections:

## Versioning and Updates

These documents may be revised from time to time by the appropriate governing body.  
When revisions occur, updates will be reflected here to ensure this site remains the most current reference.

For questions regarding interpretation or application of these documents, please consult church leadership.
