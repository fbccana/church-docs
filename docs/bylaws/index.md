# Bylaws

The Bylaws provide the operational rules by which the church functions on a day-to-day basis.

They establish procedures for decision-making, leadership responsibilities, meetings, and administration in accordance with the Constitution.

---

## Scope

The Bylaws typically address:

- Leadership roles and responsibilities  
- Church officers and committees  
- Business meetings and voting procedures  
- Financial oversight and administration  
- Amendment procedures

---

## Relationship to the Constitution

The Bylaws operate under the authority of the Constitution and may not conflict with it.  
In the event of a conflict, the Constitution shall prevail.
