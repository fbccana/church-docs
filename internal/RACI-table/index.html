    Church Office RACI Workshop: Bilingual Congregation with Multiple Ministers¶

    Date: [Insert Date]¶

    Prepared for: [Church Name]¶

    1. Role Definitions & Acronyms¶

    Secretarial & Administrative Staff¶

    Role Acronym Primary Function
    Membership Secretary MS Database management, church rolls, new member processing, directories
    Financial Secretary FS Treasury, bookkeeping, invoice processing, budget compliance
    Media/Publications Secretary MPS Bulletin, worship slides, print vendor management, graphic design, publications (Part-time)
    Receptionist/Music/Worship Secretary RMW Choir logistics, sheet music, accompanist scheduling, rehearsal coordination

    Support Staff¶

    Role Acronym Primary Function
    Family Life Center Assistant 1 FLC1 Physical logistics, building setup, deliveries, distribution, hospitality
    Family Life Center Assistant 2 FLC2 Physical logistics, building setup, deliveries, distribution, hospitality

    Ministers¶

    Role Acronym Primary Domain
    Senior Pastor SP Final authority on theology, preaching, congregational life, major expenditures
    Minister of Administration P-ADMIN Internal operations, facilities, HR, administrative workflows, operational budget
    Minister of Music/Worship P-MUSIC English worship music, liturgy, anthem selection, worship committee liaison
    Hispanic Pastor P-HISP Spanish-language ministry, Hispanic congregation pastoral care, Spanish content authority
    Youth Pastor P-YOUTH Youth ministry, programming, events, budget
    Children's Pastor P-CHILD Children's ministry, programming, events, budget

    Governance¶

    Role Acronym Primary Function
    Committee COMM Lay leadership body (Finance, Worship, Properties, Missions, etc.); researches needs, drafts proposals, recommends expenditures
    Congregation CONG Church vote; ultimate accountability authority

    2. Ministerial Accountability Matrix¶

    Use this matrix to determine which Minister serves as Accountable (A) for a given domain. Reference this when assigning RACI roles.

    Domain Accountable Minister
    Theology & Preaching (English) SP
    Theology & Preaching (Spanish) P-HISP
    Worship Music & Liturgy (English) P-MUSIC
    Worship Music & Liturgy (Spanish) P-HISP
    Youth Ministry P-YOUTH
    Children's Ministry P-CHILD
    Facilities & Properties P-ADMIN
    Administration & HR P-ADMIN
    Financial Oversight (Operational) P-ADMIN (in consultation with Finance Committee)
    Financial Oversight (Major/Capital) SP (in consultation with Finance Committee)
    Missions & Outreach SP / Committee-specific
    Hispanic Congregation Affairs P-HISP

    3. RACI Tables: Core Church Office Processes¶

    3.1 Process: Preparing the Weekly Bulletin¶

    Critical Context: Bilingual content + Outside printer + Multiple ministers + FLC physical logistics

    Task MS FS MPS RMW FLC1/2 SP P-ADMIN P-MUSIC P-HISP COMM
    Gather English Announcements R A R I C I I
    Gather Spanish Announcements A I R
    Compile & Format Layout R/A I I C
    Translate Key Content A R
    Proofread Theology/Tone (English) I C A I C
    Proofread Theology/Tone (Spanish) C I A
    Verify Financial Data R/A I I C (Finance)
    Send File to Outside Printer R/A I
    Receive Delivery from Printer I R
    Distribute to Campus Spaces A R
    Distribute to Hispanic Campus/Fellowship I C A

    Notes: - MPS is Accountable for the existence and quality of the bulletin file. - P-HISP is Accountable for Spanish theological accuracy and distribution to Hispanic sites. - SP is Accountable for English theological accuracy. - FLC1/2 are Responsible for physical receipt from printer and campus distribution. - Finance Committee is Consulted on financial data accuracy.

    3.2 Process: Worship Slides & Multilingual Content¶

    Critical Context: Multiple services (English/Spanish), potentially bilingual combined services, multiple content contributors.

    Task MPS RMW FLC1/2 SP P-MUSIC P-HISP P-YOUTH P-CHILD
    Select English Hymns/Liturgy C I R/A
    Select Spanish Hymns/Liturgy C I R/A
    Provide Sermon Outline (SP) I R
    Provide Youth Moment Content I R
    Provide Children's Message I R
    Build English Slide Deck R/A C I I
    Build Spanish Slide Deck R/A C
    Insert Bilingual Slides (if combined) R/A I C
    Tech Check/Run Booth R
    Set Up Projectors/Screens I R

    Notes: - P-MUSIC owns Accountability for English worship music selection. - P-HISP owns Accountability for Spanish worship music selection. - P-YOUTH and P-CHILD are Responsible for providing their respective content segments. - MPS is Accountable for the final slide deck(s) and technical execution. - FLC1/2 handle physical AV equipment setup (projectors, screens, cables).

    3.3 Process: Music/Choir Preparation¶

    Critical Context: Multiple worship styles (English/Spanish), two music ministers, FLC support for physical logistics.

    Task RMW MPS FLC1/2 SP P-MUSIC P-HISP COMM
    Select English Choral Anthems C I R/A C (Worship)
    Select Spanish/Latin Hymns C R/A
    Source/Order Sheet Music R/A C C
    Prepare Choir Folders (English) R R I
    Prepare Choir Folders (Spanish) R R I
    Schedule Accompanists R/A C
    Copy/Scan Copyright Licenses R R
    Set Up Choir Room/Rehearsal Space I R

    Notes: - RMW serves both P-MUSIC and P-HISP. They are Responsible for execution, but each minister is Accountable for their respective repertoire. - FLC1/2 assist with physical music preparation (folder assembly) and room setup. - Worship Committee is Consulted on anthem selection.

    3.4 Process: Adding New Members to the Church Roll¶

    Critical Context: Bilingual membership pathway, Spanish-language classes and vows, database integrity.

    Task MS FS MPS RMW FLC1/2 SP P-ADMIN P-HISP
    Receive English Membership Inquiry R I C C I
    Receive Spanish Membership Inquiry C R
    Schedule English Membership Class R R A I
    Schedule Spanish Membership Class I R R/A
    Process Membership Vows (English) R I A
    Process Membership Vows (Spanish) R I I A
    Update Database/Roll R/A I I I
    Order New Member Packets A C R
    Translate New Member Materials A R

    Notes: - MS is the sole gatekeeper of the official church roll/database (both R and A). - P-HISP owns the entire membership pathway for Spanish-speaking members (inquiry, classes, vows). - SP owns the English membership pathway and confirms vows. - FLC1/2 handle room setup for membership classes (both languages). - MPS is Accountable for translation quality of new member materials; P-HISP is Responsible for providing accurate source content.

    3.5 Process: Committee Expenditure Request¶

    Critical Context: Committee recommends, Congregation approves, multiple Ministers have delegated budget authority.

    Task FS MPS FLC1/2 SP P-ADMIN Relevant Minister COMM CONG
    Identify Need I C R
    Draft Expenditure Proposal I R/A
    Review Budget Impact R C C
    Minister Endorsement A (Major) A (Ops) A (Dept) C
    Recommend Approval I R A
    Place Order/Purchase A R I I I
    Process Invoice/Payment R/A
    Receive/Inventory Order R

    Minister Budget Accountability:

    Expenditure Type Accountable Minister
    Major capital, vision-aligned, congregation-wide SP
    Operational, facilities, administrative P-ADMIN
    Music/worship ministry P-MUSIC
    Youth ministry P-YOUTH
    Children's ministry P-CHILD
    Hispanic ministry P-HISP
    Missions (committee-driven) SP / Committee Chair

    Notes: - COMM is Responsible and Accountable for the proposal itself (quality, research, justification). - CONG is Accountable for final approval via church vote. - FS is Accountable for payment processing and budget compliance. - FLC1/2 are Responsible for receiving, inventorying, and storing physical orders/supplies.

    5. Implementation Recommendations¶

    5.1 Immediate Next Steps¶

    1. Validate Ministerial Accountability Matrix with your pastoral staff. Ensure each minister agrees to their assigned domains.

    2. Socialize the "FLC Shift" with the Family Life Center staff. Clarify that their primary RACI role is Physical Logistics they own the building; secretaries own the content.

    3. Create a "Weekly Deadline Checklist" from the Bulletin and Worship Slides RACI charts. Post this in the office and distribute to all ministers. Include:

    4. Content deadlines
    5. Who provides what
    6. Who approves what

    7. Printer cutoff times

    8. Train MPS on Vendor Management Role. The shift to outside printer means MPS is now a vendor relationship manager, not a machine operator.

    9. Establish Spanish-Language Workflow. P-HISP should formally own the process for:

    10. Spanish bulletin content
    11. Spanish worship slides
    12. Hispanic membership pathway
    13. Hispanic music selection

    5.2 Long-Term Maintenance¶

    • Keep RACI Live, Not Static. These charts should live in your Department Playbook, Wiki, or SOP documentation. Review them annually or upon staff turnover.

    • Onboarding Tool. Use these RACI charts to onboard new ministers, secretaries, and FLC staff. It answers the question: "Who do I go to for X?"

    • Don't Put RACI in Job Descriptions. Use RACI to write job descriptions, then file the charts in your operations manual.
