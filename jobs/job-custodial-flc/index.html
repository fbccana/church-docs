    Custodian - Gym/Family Life Center¶

    Department: Custodial
    Reports To: Minister of Administration
    FLSA Status: Non-Exempt
    Employment Type: Part-time
    Date: December 17, 2025

    Position Summary¶

    Responsible for the cleanliness and maintenance of the gym and family life center facilities, while serving as assistant to the Facilities Manager for various maintenance tasks and potentially developing skills for future advancement.

    Essential Functions and Responsibilities¶

    Primary Responsibilities (60-70% of time)¶

    • Maintain gym floors including sweeping, mopping, and floor scrubbing operations
    • Clean family life center areas including furniture, equipment, and surfaces
    • Clean restrooms and replenish supplies in assigned areas
    • Vacuum and maintain carpeted areas within family life center

    Secondary Responsibilities (20-30% of time)¶

    • Assist Facilities Manager with basic maintenance tasks including light replacements, minor repairs, and equipment maintenance
    • Support maintenance operations for grounds, vehicles, and building systems
    • Set up and arrange facilities for special events in family life center
    • Learn and develop maintenance skills under Facilities Manager supervision

    Additional Responsibilities (10% of time)¶

    • Assist with wedding facility arrangements when requested (extra compensation per church policy)
    • Coordinate with Facilities Manager for cleaning and maintenance supply needs
    • Other duties as assigned by Minister of Administration

    Qualifications¶

    Required¶

    • High school diploma or equivalent
    • Basic mechanical aptitude and willingness to learn maintenance skills
    • Ability to operate floor scrubbing equipment and custodial machinery
    • Physical ability to lift up to 50 pounds and perform manual labor
    • Flexible schedule accommodating second job responsibilities

    Preferred¶

    • Experience with building maintenance, HVAC, plumbing, or electrical systems
    • Custodial or janitorial experience
    • Interest in developing facilities management skills

    Physical Requirements and Working Conditions¶

    • Extended periods of standing, walking, bending, and lifting
    • Operation of floor scrubbing and cleaning equipment
    • Indoor work environment with occasional outdoor maintenance tasks
    • Preferred schedule: Monday-Friday, 4 hours per day (flexible based on availability)
    • Ability to work independently and as part of maintenance team

    Performance Expectations¶

    • Maintain gym and family life center to high cleanliness standards
    • Demonstrate reliability and consistency in part-time schedule
    • Show initiative in learning maintenance skills and procedures
    • Support special events with efficient setup and cleanup

    Reporting Relationships¶

    Direct Reports: None
    Key Internal Relationships: Facilities Manager, Lead Custodian, Ministry Staff
    External Relationships: Equipment Vendors, Maintenance Contractors

    Compensation and Benefits¶

    Hourly wage as determined by Personnel Committee and approved in annual church budget. Additional compensation available for wedding assistance per church policy.

    Employment Conditions¶

    • Position continues until terminated by mutual agreement or by request of Minister of Administration
    • Two weeks' notice required for resignation unless otherwise mutually agreed
    • Subject to successful completion of background check
    • Compliance with church policies and procedures required
    • At-will employment relationship

    Reasonable Accommodations¶

    First Baptist Church will make reasonable accommodations to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform essential functions of this position.
